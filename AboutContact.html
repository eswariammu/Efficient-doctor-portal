About Us

Patients and families from across the region and around the world come to Yale New Haven Children's Hospital for its ongoing commitment to exceptional patient care. Our physicians treat a wide array of illnesses, including hard-to-treat cancers and metabolic disorders, and use techniques to support infants and children with severe diseases when they do not respond to conventional therapies.
